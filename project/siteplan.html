DICE ROLLER SITEPLAN

1. Site Name

The site name will be "D&D Dice Roller". It's very simple and to the point. It's a dice roller to be used for table-top roleplaying games, but I'll call it "D&D Dice Roller" because D&D is the most common TTRPG.

2. Site Purpose

As previously stated, it will be a dice-rolling application for TTRPGS.
I may add additional function and a secondary page to do character stat rolling for Dungeons & Dragons specifically. My main focus is to roll dice and have a visual representation of the dice with the number of the roll printed on the dice.

3. Scenarios

1. What is the result of rolling 4 6-sided dice?

2. What is the result of rolling 1 20-sided die and 2 6-sided dice?

4. Color Schema

The background color will be background-color: #d7ff69;. It's green but not overwhelming. The font will remain black for legibility. Any accents, borders, or lines on the page will be brown in color. I will have a div box where svgs of dice are placed as to represent each dice being rolled, and the interior of that box will be a lighter brown color.

5. Typography

I plan on using the font "Almendra" for the headings. I'm still deciding on a font for all other text, but I may just remain with Times New Roman.
